# Progression Notes

This pack aims for a nostalgic, chill Minecraft 1.12.2 tech progression.

## Current Direction

- IndustrialCraft 2 is the early backbone.
- Thermal Expansion and TeslaCoreLib components are gated behind IC2-oriented infrastructure.
- Industrial Foregoing is a gated automation layer rather than an early all-in-one shortcut.
- Draconic Evolution and GraviSuite tools are treated as late-game milestones.

## Materials

- Silver is a rare worldgen metal. It also appears as a small IC2 gold ore processing byproduct, so gold processing is a useful bonus path rather than the only source of silver.
- Platinum is not intended as a mining path. The intended route is Molecular Transformer gold to platinum; platinum ingots and derived materials remain usable.

## Ore Processing

- The IC2 Macerator is the intended first ore-doubling and dust-processing machine.
- Certus Quartz Dust, Nether Quartz Dust, Fluix Dust, and Sky Stone Dust are produced with the IC2 Macerator; the AE2 Quartz Grindstone and Wooden Crank are disabled.
- The Railcraft Rock Crusher is limited to selected building-material conversions and Worldspike salvage. It does not process ores, processed ores, ingots, coal, or general grinder inputs.

## Steel

- Steel is produced through the IC2 Blast Furnace path.
- The Railcraft Blast Furnace is disabled to avoid duplicate steel progression.
- Railcraft remains available for the Coke Oven, rolling machines, rails, tanks, infrastructure, and flavor.

## Circuits

- IC2 circuits remain the intended circuit gate.
- Energy Control's cheaper Basic and Advanced Circuit Kit Assembler recipes are removed and those circuit items are hidden, preventing them from weakening progression gates.
